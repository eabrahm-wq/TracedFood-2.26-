01
The Playbook

A founder builds a brand with genuine values. PE firm invests at growth stage â€” providing capital for distribution and marketing. The brand scales rapidly. Then either: (a) the PE firm sells to a conglomerate, or (b) holds and quietly optimizes margins.

02

PE investment is not inherently bad â€” but it's a leading indicator. Watch for: rapid SKU expansion, reduced ingredient quality, "family of brands" language appearing on packaging, sudden distribution into mass-market chains.
